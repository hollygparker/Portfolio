about me

I am an aspiring developer who pivoted into the tech industry in 2021. While working in fintech support, I discovered that I am a fast learner with an interest in developing programming skills. I have done a fair amount of self-learning via Codecademy and Udemy and have decided to make the commitment to this bootcamp. My knowledge of HTML and CSS have already helped incredibly with certain issues I need to troubleshoot at work, and I'm excited to absorb even more knowledge.

I live in Chicago with my wife Jessica, and our two cats, Luna and Milo. We have lived here since 2017 and are happy to call Chicago home. I am obsessed with urban sketching, amphibians of all shapes and sizes, and trivia (just don't ask me about sports). A fun fact about me is that my brain is 95% Lizzie McGuire Movie references.
